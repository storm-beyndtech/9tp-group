F. A. Q.

Do you have additional questions?

What is the approximate team size, budget, and time frame for custom chatbot development?

Similarly to other software solutions, everything depends on the complexity of your project. Simple custom chatbots may be created and deployed in less than a month with one skilled developer and one QA engineer, and cost around $7500. The cost of advanced bots may go well beyond $22500 and require a team of five and two-three months of development. Still, these are only approximate numbers and the final costs will depend on the specifics of your project.

What types of chatbot exist today?

There are two main types of chatbot — text based and voiced based. Both have two subtypes: straightforward rule-based chatbots that can only address simple queries based on keywords and simple user behaviour patterns; and more sophisticated chatbots that use machine learning algorithms and natural language processing to tackle more complex questions.

What are the best chatbot development platforms?

It is hard to pinpoint one or two platforms. The best chatbot platform for your project will be the one that fit its requirements the most.
