CLIENT SUCCESS

Join an incredible record of organizations, from startups to Fortune 1000, who trust our mobile application development services.

GUINNESS

A marketing app for Arthur Guinness Day vitalized the conversation between the beloved beverage brand and its admirers.

Challenge

Guinness needed a useful tool for gathering customer data that would double as a compelling brand experience and a reminder of their historical and cultural roots.

Solution

We created an app based on St. Patrick's Day. A series of mini-games and topical quizzes led customers to interact with their favorite stout, providing valuable data about their drinking preferences. Our team also created a crisp, UI landscape view.

JAGUAR

Intellectsoft helps Jaguar leap over language barriers in conference calls.

Challenge

Jaguar Land Rover commissioned Intellectsoft to develop an effortless way of advancing communication for the company’s representatives from different language groups.

Solution

Intellectsoft created a multi-language iPad app with an intuitive interface that covers all of the company’s communication needs. The users can perform conference calls with up to 5 simultaneous conferences, with each participant communicating freely in their native tongue.

CIRRUS INSIGHT

Cirruspath is a cross-platform SaaS company specializing in apps that integrate Salesforce CRM tools with email.

Challenge

The client wanted to develop a mobile app that would extend the capabilities of their Salesforce integration to iOS. This would require a team to develop an API that retained existing features as well as optimize additional functions.

Solution

We created a comprehensive mobile email client with built-in, advanced CRM functionality. The Cirrus Insight app is native and connects to the Cirrus Salesforce-integrated platform via a private API.

HARLEY-DAVIDSON

The iconic motorcycle brand finds a fresh way to engage its fans while on the road by creating a mobile app that allows exhibition staff to sign prospective clients.

Challenge

Together with Harley-Davidson, we set out to develop a mobile app that would help brand reps facilitate lead conversion when interacting with customers at biking events.

Solution

We created an app for their exhibition staff, helping brand reps visually showcase product customizations through a "user mentor" feature that functions in more than 15 languages.

ERNST & YOUNG

Forecasts in Focus confirms E&Y as a thought-leader in the eyes of an audience relying on data to drive smarter investment decisions.

Challenge

E&Y needed to reinforce its reputation for thought leadership by providing financial professionals with a consistent stream of expert opinions and education.

Solution

The resulting mobile solution was given the name Forecasts in Focus — and designed to deliver value at each end.

EUROSTAR

A high-speed railway pioneer gains a series of automated solutions to process passenger information, improve on-board services, and report critical information to passengers.

Challenge

Eurostar required web and mobile applications to automate its business-critical ERP processes and enable further convenience for their customers.

Solution

Intellectsoft developed an employee-facing cloud solution for web and iOS named Eurostar iTM.
