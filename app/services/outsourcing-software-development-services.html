F. A. Q.

Do you have additional questions?

What are the benefits of outsourcing software development in 2020 and beyond?

Emerging technologies like augmented reality and machine learning become more effective and popular. As a result, hiring, managing, and retaining the right experts while sticking to the set time frame becomes increasingly complex and heavy on the budget. Software development outsourcing helps companies tackle this and other challenges, allowing them to deliver high-quality software solutions on time.

What outsourcing software development services are available today?

The types of outsourcing services remain the same — dedicated team and staff augmentation, as well as other types similar to them. They include team extension and managed services, but every company that offers these services approaches them differently.

What factors are key in the process of selecting a software development outsourcing company/partner?

First and foremost, how many years a company has been offering these services. If you need a team for a small to medium project, seek out companies with at least five years on the market under the belt. If you have a big long-term project that involves multiple advanced technologies, choose among companies with at least ten years. Second, look into their past and ongoing projects. Finally, ensure a company has a comprehensive approach to software development where every step is clear. These are the most important factors.

What is a software development outsourcing methodology?

It is the way a company approaches and provides their software development outsourcing services. This includes approaches to hiring, scaling, supporting the client, and so on.
