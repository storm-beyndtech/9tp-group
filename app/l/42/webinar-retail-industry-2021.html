About the webinar

The retail environment keeps changing impetuously as consumers began to alter their buying behaviour, shifting to digital channels, products, and services, which opened up entirely new fronts for retail businesses to recover after the pandemic. Businesses and consumers increasingly “went digital”, providing and purchasing more goods and services online, raising e-commerce’s share of global retail trade to about 17% in 2020.

Join the webinar to explore what strategies retail business leaders must adopt to benefit from changes in consumer behavior and drive revenue growth in 2021 and beyond.

During this exclusive webinar you will:

  • Discover how major technology predictions and key market trends are reshaping the future of the retail industry.
  • Gain valuable strategic insights on why leading retailers are adopting cutting-edge technologies to position themselves for success in 2021 and beyond.
  • Learn from real-life cases of how international retail companies delivered digital products and services fast with the help of tech solutions.
  • Find answers to the most pressing questions on how to respond to shifts in consumer behavior and deliver a unified retail commerce experience for customers.
